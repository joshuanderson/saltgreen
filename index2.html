Based in Swindon we provide a range of trade services to domestic and commercial customers both locally and within the surrounding area.

We have over 30 years of trade experience and have built in that time a reputation for being friendly, trustworthy and driven by a passion for excellent customer service.

We know how stressful and frustrating it can be to have your home or place of work in a state of disarray whilst building or maintenance work is carried out. For that reason we aim to provide a flexible service which will keep any disruption to you at an absolute minimum. We will work with you to provide a service that fits with your life whether at work or at home.

Saltgreen Carpentry & Joinery was founded by Steve Anderson in 2002. Since its origin the company has consistently provided high quality services to home owners and Swindon businesses, including local council and schools.

Steve has many years of experience in the construction industry and has in that time tackled an array of challenging building problems. Whilst Steve's specialism is carpentry and joinery he has a wide knowledge of all aspects of building and maintenance work and works collaboratively with skilled trade professionals to deliver a thorough and reputable service.

At Saltgreen we offer a wide range of services and can supply and fit all materials for projects such as door hanging, fitting or refurbishing kitchens, garage conversions and fitting UPVC doors and windows.

We also undertake bespoke carpentry projects, for example the design and crafting of new church gates and unusual baby stair gates.
