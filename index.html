A friendly, trustworthy and reliable service
that delivers quality workmanship

We are carpentry and joinery specialists proudly based in Swindon, providing a range of high quality trade services to domestic and commercial customers both locally and in the surrounding areas.

With over 30 years of trade experience and hundreds of satisfied customers, we have a reputation for providing friendly, trustworthy and reliable service. We pride ourselves on our personal approach to every project, and we hold ourselves to incredibly high standards.

It can be both stressful and frustrating to have your home or workplace in a state of disarray whilst building or maintenance work is carried out, and for this reason, we aim to provide you with a flexible and convenient service that will keep disruption to an absolute minimum. Whether you need our help at work or at home, we'll work hard to ensure that our service fits your schedule and your lifestyle.

Saltgreen Carpentry and Joinery was founded by Steve Anderson in 2002. Since the very beginning, we have consistently provided high quality service to home owners, businesses and organisations in Swindon, including schools and the local council.

We have extensive experience in the construction industry and have successfully tackled a wide range of challenging building needs over the years. Though we are carpentry and joinery specialists, we have a wide knowledge of all aspects of building and maintenance work and have always collaborated with skilled trade professionals to deliver a thorough and reputable service that you can trust.

We offer a wide range of services and have decades of experience in all areas of building and maintenance, with 20 years specialist experience with doors. We can supply and fit all materials for the project you're interested in, whether that's door hanging, fitting or refurbishing a kitchen, shelving, wood window repairs or garage conversions.

We also undertake bespoke carpentry projects, such as the design and crafting of new gates and fences and unusual baby stair gates. In addition, we provide a fire door service check for school, academies and homes, so we're best placed to keep you and your family safe and sound.
